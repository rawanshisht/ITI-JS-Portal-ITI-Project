Subero

People develop countries, we develop P.E.O.P.L.E

Subero is a website to help iTi students organize their rich journey. First, you should register in our website to be able to open your profile page which includes 3 pages. One is to-do list page where you can add tasks and check when they are done or delete them if they are canceled. Also, their is courses page where there are many links to courses and you can check the syllabus. Last but not least, we all neeed a break from study, so their is a memory game to have a break and study again. Contact us page is to email us with your problem. We hope you enjoy our website.
